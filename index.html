Our Story

Three generations.
One passion.

Mario Conti arrived from Naples in 1987 with a worn recipe book and an unshakable belief that great pizza begins with great flour, great fire, and great heart. Today, his grandchildren carry that same torch — same dough, same stone oven, same obsession.
